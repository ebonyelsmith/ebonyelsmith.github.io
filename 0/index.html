Project 0: Becoming Friends with Your Camera

The goal of this project is to get some intuitive understanding of the subtle relationship between perspective, focal length/zoom, and the center of projection.

Part 1: Selfie: The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

The second image looks much better than the first image because the zoom has a flattening effect on the subject that does not place as much focus on the center of the face compared to the first image.

The selfie is taken without zoom from closer up.

The selfie is taken with a 2x zoom from further away.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

Following the same instructions as the first part with an urban scene, we see the reverse effect. The zoomed in photo is more compressed and less quality than the photo taken up close without as much zoom.
